Example 1: Effect of Three Substrate Sterilization Methods on the Production of Pleurotus ostreatus

##Bojective: 
evaluate the effect of three wheat straw substrate sterilization methods on production of ostreatus variety of Florida,and to exmine which method produces the most mushroom yield. 

##BackGround:
The goal is to evaluate the effect of three substrate sterilization methods (wheat straw) on the production of Pleurotus ostreatus variety Florida. 
 3 types of substrate sterilization
steam: prior soaking of the wheat straw for 12 hours, after which the straw is placed in the sterilizer. 50 liters of water are added to the bottom of the sterilizer, heated to 90°C, and maintained at this temperature for 80 minutes. The straw is then cooled and drained to leave it with a moisture content of 70%.
immersion in water:1000 liters of water are heated to 90°C in a stainless steel tank. Once this temperature is reached, the wheat straw is added, and the tank is sealed under pressure and sterilized at 100°C for 60 minutes. After this, the straw is cooled and drained to achieve 70% moisture content. 1000 liters of water are heated to 90°C in a stainless steel tank. Once this temperature is reached, the wheat straw is added, and the tank is sealed under pressure and sterilized at 100°C for 60 minutes. After this, the straw is cooled and drained to achieve 70% moisture content
. Semi-composting and pasteurization: the wheat straw is moistened to 80% and kept at that moisture level for 5 days, during which it is aerated twice a day. Afterward, the semi-composted straw is placed in the pasteurization chamber, where it remains for 10 hours at 65°C. After this, the straw is allowed to cool and is drained to a moisture content of 70%.

##Design:
-Factour: Sterilization Methods (3 types - Vapour, Submerged and semicomposting)
-Response Variable: Mushroom Production in Kg
-Sample Size: n=45 per group totelling to N=135
-Design: One way ANOVA

##Statistical Analysis
- One-way ANOVA
- Tukey HSD post-hoc test for all pairwise comparisons
- Assumption checks: Shapiro-Wilk normality test (per group),
  Levene homogeneity of variance test
